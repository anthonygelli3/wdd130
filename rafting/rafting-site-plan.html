Created by Anthony Gelli for WDD 130

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote Tony Tour’s rafting adventures in Ghana. It will allow potential customers to learn more about our tours, team, and safety, and get excited to book an unforgettable experience on the water.

Audience

Our target audience is adventure-loving tourists, families, and youth groups looking for a fun and safe outdoor activity in Ghana. The site will be easy to navigate, mobile-friendly, and include exciting images of rafting and team bonding.
