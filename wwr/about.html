Our History

Tony Tour was founded in 2010 by a passionate group of river enthusiasts who wanted to share the thrill of white-water rafting with the world. What began as weekend adventures along local rivers has grown into a professional and exciting rafting experience known across the region.

Our mission is to create unforgettable moments for families, tourists, and thrill-seekers. With over a decade of experience, Tony Tour continues to deliver safe, fun, and memorable outdoor adventures.
